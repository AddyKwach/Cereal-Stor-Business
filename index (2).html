Our Story

More Than a Shop.
A Nairobi Institution.

GrainHaus was founded with a single mission: to connect Nairobi's homes, restaurants, and businesses with the freshest, highest-quality cereals and pulses that East Africa has to offer.

We source directly from trusted Kenyan farmers, ensuring every grain you buy is traceable, clean, and packed with nutrition. Whether you need a small bag for the week or a bulk supply for your business — we've got you covered.
