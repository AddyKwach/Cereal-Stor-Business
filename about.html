Who We Are

More Than a Shop.
A Nairobi Institution.

GrainHaus was founded with a single mission: to connect Nairobi's homes, restaurants, and businesses with the freshest, highest-quality cereals and pulses that East Africa has to offer.

We started as a small stall at Wakulima Market, building relationships with farmers from across Kenya — from the fertile hills of Trans Nzoia to the maize plains of Rift Valley. Over the years, those relationships have grown into a trusted supply network spanning over 50 grain varieties.

Today, GrainHaus serves over 1,200 customers — from individual families buying weekly groceries to hotels, schools, and businesses ordering tonnes at a time. But our values remain the same: quality, fairness, and community.

Our Values

What We Stand For

01

Traceability

Every grain is traceable back to a verified Kenyan farm. No anonymous imports — only trusted local sourcing.

02

Fairness

Fair prices for our customers, fair pay for our farmers. We believe the supply chain should benefit everyone in it.

03

Quality

Every bag is cleaned, sorted, and graded before it reaches you. No compromise, no shortcuts.

04

Community

GrainHaus is proudly Kenyan. We invest in local farmers, employ locally, and give back to Nairobi.
